How to Join

Joining Project LA 1995 is simple and handled through our email system. To begin, join the Project LA Emails Discord and submit a whitelisted application. Your application helps us understand your RP style and ensures that every player entering the city contributes to a high‑quality, immersive environment. Applications are reviewed within a maximum of 24 hours, and you will receive a response through the email system once your application has been accepted or if any changes are needed.

Server Rules

Before entering the city, all players must read the server rules in full. These rules exist to maintain fairness, protect the RP environment, and ensure that everyone has an enjoyable experience. Failure to follow the rules may result in points being added to your account, which act as warnings. Accumulating too many points can lead to temporary suspensions or permanent bans depending on the severity of the rulebreak. We strongly recommend reviewing the rules carefully and checking back for updates whenever they are announced.

Roleplay Guide

Project LA 1995 is built on immersive, story‑driven roleplay. To help you integrate smoothly, here are the core RP tools and systems you’ll use:

/me — Used to describe your character’s visible actions.
Example: /me pats the suspect down for weapons

/do — Used to describe information others would realistically know or see.
Example: /do You would see a wallet in his back pocket.

Applying for LEO: If you want to join LAPD, CHP, or any law enforcement agency, you must apply through the Emails. You can gain access via the Emails Tab. All LEO roles are whitelisted and require an application to ensure professionalism and RP quality.

Jobs & City Hall: Civilians can obtain jobs at City Hall, Only including public work. To get employed to a whitelisted position, Whitelisted jobs require approval — simply contact the server owner or the appropriate department lead to apply.
